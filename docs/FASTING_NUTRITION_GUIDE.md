## Fasting + Training Notes

### Friday Plyometrics (Morning)
⚠️ **Warning:** You're doing plyometrics before eating (fasted state)
- **Risk:** Increased muscle loss in fasted state
- **Options:**
  1. Move Friday plyos to afternoon (after lunch)
  2. Have a small snack before (half shake or banana)
  3. Continue fasted but accept slightly more muscle loss risk

**Recommendation:** Move plyos to afternoon if possible, or have quick pre-workout snack.

### Other Sessions
- Gym sessions (Mon-Fri): Okay fasted or fed, but better with some calories
- Cardio: Works fasted (zone 2 is low intensity)

## Tips for Success

1. **Meal Prep:** Cook chicken/meat in batches (3-4x/week)
2. **Consistency:** Same portions = easier to hit targets
3. **Timing:** Lunch 11am-1pm, Snack 3-4pm, Dinner 7-8pm (example)
4. **Hunger:** If too hungry, add vegetables (low calorie, high volume)
5. **HSN Shake:** Mix with water for lower calories, milk for creamier/more calories
